About Me

Hello! I'm Yeboah Solomon Opoku, the founder and CEO of ShopEasy. With over 5 years of experience in -commerce and retail technology, I've always been passionate about creating seamless shopping experiences for customers.

I started ShopEasy with a simple mission: to make online shopping more accessible, enjoyable, and trustworthy. Our platform brings together the best products from verified sellers, ensuring quality and customer satisfaction with every purchase.

When I'm not working on improving ShopEasy, you can find me exploring new technologies, reading about consumer behavior, or spending time with my family. I believe that technology should enhance our lives, and that's exactly what we strive to do at ShopEasy.
